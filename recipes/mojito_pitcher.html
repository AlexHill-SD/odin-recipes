Mojito Pitcher

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rum
  • 1 liter club soda
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 36 leaves fresh mint, or to taste
  • 3 limes, quartered

Recipe

  • Muddle the sugar and mint leaves together in a pitcher.
    • >
  • Once the mint leaves have broken down, add the lime and muddle until all juice is extracted
  • Add in the rum, stir to combine.
  • Top up with club soda.
