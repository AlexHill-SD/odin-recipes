Pineapple Sangria

Ingredients

  • 1.5 litres Sauvignon Blanc
  • 2 cups Pineapple Juice
  • 1 cup Triple Sec
  • 1/2 cup Brandy
  • 2 cups Sprite
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1/2 cup chopped orange
  • 1 chopped, & seeded, lemon
  • 1/4 cup chopped lime

Recipe

  • Mix all the liquids in a large pitcher.
    • >
  • Add the chopped fruit and stir to combine.
  • Cover and refigerate, 8+ hours if possible.
